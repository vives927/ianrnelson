It is normal to be hesitant about seeking treatment. We often feel like we should be able to handle life on our own. It can be helpful, however, to have a mental health check-up. Many people are relieved to find that others can relate to their experience and that psychotherapy can foster both relief from current symptoms and strength to live life more fully.

If you are experiencing any symptoms related to anxiety, stress, depression, trauma, PTSD or concerns about substance use, it could be time to contact myself or another professional for a consultation and assessment. In addition, many relationship and spiritual concerns can be explored in therapy, offering you a safe place to be your true self and work through the fears and questions we all have in common. Finally, I offer support to those preparing for cross-cultural work outside the U.S. and those transitioning from cross-cultural work. Working through culture shock and re-adjusting to life in the states is a formidable challenge.
