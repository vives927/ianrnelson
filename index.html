Costs

How much does therapy or counseling cost?

If you have insurance coverage for outpatient psychotherapy, you might be responsible for a copay between $20 and $50 (just an estimate). Other plans require you to pay your deductible first. Call your insurance company to learn about your coverage, your copay and if there is a limit on sessions. The most helpful phone number is the one on the back of your insurance card. If you do not have insurance, a sliding scale from $50-100 is available based on your income.

For more information on finding low cost options visit http://psychcentral.com/lib/2007/finding-low-cost-psychotherapy/.
